The Squad Story

Who are we?

The Salty Paddlers is a hard-working Sprint Canoe squad comprised of junior and senior athletes on the Northern Beaches. We employ innovative and progressive elements to our training schedule. We commit to training morning and evening on Narrabeen lakes throughout the entire year. We train hard. We commit to every session, and we push each other and support each other throughout the entire process. Team camaraderie and loyalty is important to us. We go above executing the physical demands of the training program and we fuse influences paramount to successful training including understanding nutrition, mindfulness, and mobility off the water.

Our Coach Nick Dawe provides us with an exemplary training program, for both training on the water and in the Gym, that has been heavily researched and understands the relative macro and micro cycles necessary for reaching our full potential. His substantial knowledge base and passion for the sport keeps us all heavily motivated and provides the fun, progressive atmosphere at training!

We push ourselves beyond our limits, and The Salty Paddlers is built from athletes who never give up. We accept athletes of all capabilities, and we work together as each of us strives to reach our individual goals. The Salty Paddlers have a lot of fun training and competing! We compete at the various Sprint Series, GP and National events, with many of us aiming for Australian Selection. We all also rock a good Zoot Suit!
